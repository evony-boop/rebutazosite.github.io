Putting God first in my life helps me make better decisions and reminds me to always choose what is right. “Una ang Ginoo” means trusting God in everything I do, whether in school, online, or in my daily actions. As the Light of the World, I believe that even through technology, I can spread kindness, honesty, and respect. This project shows that when faith guides our use of technology, we can inspire others and create positive change in the digital world.
